root relatedContent Related Content weight
Type: number

A number between -1.0 and 1.0 indicating both how closely related these two pieces of content are, and which one it is preferable to encounter first. A value of 1.0 indicates that these two pieces of content are very closely related, and this one should be read first, and the linked one second. A value of -1.0 indicates again that these two pieces of content are very closely related, but that the linked one should be read first. A value of 0.0 indicates that these two pieces of content have nothing to do with each other.

Value must be greater or equal to -1.0 and lesser or equal to 1.0
